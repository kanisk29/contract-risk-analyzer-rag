Clause Type: Non-Compete (India)

Legal Basis:
- Indian Contract Act (Restraint of Trade)

Risk Indicators:
- Restricts working after contract ends
- Broad scope (industry-wide)
- Long duration

Legal Insight:
- Post-contract non-compete is generally unenforceable in India

Risk Level:
- VERY HIGH

Safer Alternative:
- Use non-solicitation instead
- Limit restriction during contract only
