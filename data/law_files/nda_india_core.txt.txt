Clause Type: NDA (India)

Legal Basis:
- Governed by Indian Contract Act, 1872
- Must satisfy: offer, acceptance, consideration, free consent

Key Requirements:
- Clear definition of confidential information
- Defined obligations of receiving party
- Duration of agreement
- Exclusions (public info, prior knowledge)

Risk Indicators:
- Vague definition of confidential information
- No duration specified
- No consequences of breach
- Overly broad restrictions

Risk Level:
- HIGH if terms are vague or overly restrictive

Safer Alternative:
- Clearly define scope of confidential information
- Add reasonable duration
- Specify remedies for breach
