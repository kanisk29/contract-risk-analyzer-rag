Clause Type: Offer & Acceptance

Legal Basis:
- Sections 4, 5, 7 Indian Contract Act

Key Rules:
- Acceptance must be absolute and unqualified
- Must be communicated
- Can be revoked before completion

Risk Indicators:
- Conditional acceptance
- Silence treated as acceptance incorrectly
- Improper communication

Risk Level:
- MEDIUM

Safer Alternative:
- Ensure explicit and unconditional acceptance
